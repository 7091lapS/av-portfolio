Guardian Map Tool

In addition to my editorial work, I regularly contribute to the development of tools for the production of journalism by the Guardian’s Visuals team, as well as other editorial desks.

An example of this is the software I built to simplify and speed up the creation of maps at the Guardian. This is now the Guardian's main map-making tool, used daily in newsrooms across three continents.

The tool allows graphic artists and journalists to create a map for any chosen area in the globe, at various levels of detail, and export it as svg (to be further edited in Illustrator), or as ready-made html embed (that/which can be used directly in our articles). Over time I have kept adding new features, such as support for importing external Geojson layers and custom annotations.
